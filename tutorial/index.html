step 1: plan!

this step's pretty easy. review your project, think about the steps YOU take to make it work on your machine, and make a note of a few things:

  • what software does your project need to run? what runtime?
  • are there any dependencies that need to be installed and configured? do they need specific environment variables set?
  • does your project connect to anything external? does it need specific ports exposed for connections?
  • what are your project's storage needs? are you going to need to set up separate volumes?

consider all of these pretty carefully and make notes. they'll determine how you write your Dockerfile!
