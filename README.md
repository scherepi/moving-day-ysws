# Moving Day - A Hack Club YSWS about packing old projects into containers for the future.

## How it Works:
Spend 2 hours writing a Dockerfile for an old project, or build a small project from scratch to containerize. Build a working
container, publish it to the Container Registry, and receive either $10 towards storage and hosting or a sick custom trinket made by yours truly ;)

## Blurb
Hi, I'm Guac, and I just moved from Austin, Texas to Burlington, Vermont to work for Hack Club as a gap year. That meant I had to
pack up the last 10 years of my life to bring them with me into this totally crazy new place, and I want you to join me! Ever heard
of Docker? It's a system that lets you package entire projects into self-contained little sandboxes called containers, where they
can be easily run and managed on any machine. The same way I packed up my guitar, hacking books, and sewing machine to bring to
Burlington, I want you to box up your old projects into containers and bring them into the future! If you do, I'll give you hosting
credits or a cool little doodad!!
